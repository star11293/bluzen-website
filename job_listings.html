Registered Nurse (RN)

Description: Deliver patient-centered care, coordinate with interdisciplinary teams, and manage complex medical conditions in fast-paced environments.

Responsibilities:
- Administer medications and treatments
- Monitor patient progress and update charts
- Educate patients and families on care plans

Qualifications:
- Active RN license
- BLS/ACLS certified
- 1-2 years of clinical experience
